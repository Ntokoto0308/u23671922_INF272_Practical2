Biography and Background

Tshami Mongwe was born in Tzaneen and completed their schooling at Pretoria. Growing up, Tshami was always fascinated by numbers and patterns, which sparked an interest in mathematics. This passion for numbers led Tshami to pursue a degree in BCom Statistics and Data Science. Tshami believes that the world is becoming increasingly data-driven, and with this degree, they aim to combine statistical analysis with technological innovations to solve real-world business problems. Tshami is particularly interested in how data science can improve decision-making processes in companies, from understanding consumer behavior to optimizing business strategies. Their goal is to work in a field where they can contribute to making data-based decisions that positively impact both the business world and society as a whole.

Hobbies

Aside from studying, Tshami enjoys playing soccer, reading about new technological advancements, and participating in community service activities. They also love experimenting with data visualization tools in their spare time to better understand complex data sets.
